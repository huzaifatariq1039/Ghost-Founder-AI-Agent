# Ghost-Founder-AI-Agent

1. The Cognitive Stack (Multi-Model Orchestration)
Instead of one LLM, we will use a "Council of Experts" in n8n 2.0:

The Strategist (Claude Opus 4.6): This model handles the high-level profile audit and weekly strategy. It has the deepest reasoning for professional branding and "ambiguous" creative direction.

The Archivist (Gemini 3.1 Pro): With its massive context window, this agent "reads" your entire technical history—your PulseQ pitch decks, GDG reports, research papers, and Pashto GPT documentation—ensuring every post is technically sound and contextually aware.

The Speedster (Llama 4 on Groq): Handles high-volume, low-latency tasks like real-time comment replies and sentiment analysis.

The Visualist (Nano Banana 2): Powering the image-to-image "remixing" to keep your personal brand authentic.

2. Deep Personal Knowledge Integration (RAG-less Context)
The agent will not just use search; it will have a permanent Context Vault:

Portfolio Ingestion: The system will sync with your project folders. When it writes about "AI in Healthcare," it pulls specific data points from your startup's hospital onboarding experience at Rufaydah Medical Complex.

Tone Cloning: The agent will analyze your past successful posts to learn your specific "engineer-entrepreneur" voice—avoiding the generic "AI slop" that is flooding feeds in 2026.

3. The Authentic Visual Pipeline
We will strictly avoid "uncanny valley" stock photos.

Authentic Remixing: The agent pulls your real photos and uses Nano Banana 2’s Image-Edit capability to place you in high-fidelity professional settings (e.g., creating a featured image for a LinkedIn article that looks like a high-end magazine shoot of you in a lab or a tech hub).

Brand Cohesion: All visuals will be auto-graded against a "Style Guide" to ensure consistent colors, fonts, and "vibe."

4. n8n 2.0 Agentic Workflow
We will build this using a Three-Agent Loop:

Researcher Agent: Scrapes trending tech news on X and cross-references it with your project goals.

Creative Agent: Drafts the post (text + visual) and suggests a platform-specific strategy (e.g., "Thread on X, Carousel on LinkedIn").

Validator Agent (Human-in-the-loop):

Checks for "Shadow-ban" risks (simulating human dwell time).

Verifies technical facts.

Sends a High-Fidelity Preview to your WhatsApp for a one-click !Approve.

5. Autonomous Engagement & Lead Management
Proactive Community Manager: The agent doesn't just reply; it initiates conversations with key players in the "AI in Space" or "HealthTech" niche.

Smart Triage: * Low-Value Comments: Llama 4 replies with witty, brief engagement.

High-Value (Investors/Potential Clients): The agent drafts a personalized DM response and sends you a Priority Notification on WhatsApp with a "Who's Who" summary of the person.
